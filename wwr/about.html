AMANI Rafting Association Company (ARAC)

AMANI Rafting Association company is one of the company for maintaining the happiness of people through trips in rivers. Our mission is to make the society feel more comfortable with water. Enjoy your life together with Rafting Company.

History

AMANI Rafting Association Company Started experiencing trips since 2021 after the outbreak of COVID-19. It helped from different parts of the world to use water for tavelling at every corner of the world. Within 5 years of experience AMANI Rafting Association Company built confidence and improved transport for many parts of the world.
